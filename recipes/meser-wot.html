meser wot Recipe

DESCRIPTION

meser wot is Ethiopian daily food. It is delicious and eaten with injera which is ethiopian pancake like food. The recipe is the simplest recipe and shown below.

INGREDIENTS

Ingredients that are required

  • Onion
  • oil
  • pepper
  • Garlic
  • lentils
  • water
  • salt
  • and habesha's butter

STEPS

Steps to make a delicious meser wot

  1. first put the onion on utensil after chopping
  2. after that fry it with oil and add pepper
  3. after you fry it with pepper and the garlic
  4. then add the lentils
  5. then add water and let it be cooked
  6. finally add the salt and butter
  7. the mesr wot is ready to eat
