Dench-wot Recipe

DESCRIPTION

Dench-wot is Ethiopian daily food. It is delicious and eaten with injera which is ethiopian pancake like food. The recipe is the simplest recipe and shown below.

INGREDIENTS

Ingredients that are required

  • Onion
  • oil
  • pepper
  • tomato
  • Garlic
  • potatoes
  • water
  • salt

STEPS

Steps to make a delicious Dench-wot

  1. first put the onion on utensil after chopping
  2. after that fry it with oil and add pepper
  3. after you fry it with pepper add the tomato and the garlic
  4. then add the potatoes
  5. add water and let the potato be cooked
  6. finally add the salt
  7. the Dench wot is ready to eat
