Shiro Recipe

DESCRIPTION

Shiro is Ethiopian daily food. It is delicious and eaten with injera which is ethiopian pancake like food. The shiro recipe is the simplest recipe and shown below.

INGREDIENTS

Ingredients that are required

  • Onion
  • oil
  • pepper
  • tomato
  • Garlic
  • water
  • shiro powder
  • salt
  • chili
  • and habesha's butter

STEPS

Steps to make a delicious shiro

  1. first put the onion on utensil after chopping
  2. after that fry it with oil and add pepper
  3. after you fry it with pepper add the tomato and the garlic
  4. then add enough water
  5. then add enough shiro powder until it become like a sauce
  6. finally add the chili and the salt
  7. the shiro is ready to eat
